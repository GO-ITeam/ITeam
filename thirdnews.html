Threats impacting forest health

By Laura Olivieri Aug. 20, 2015 , 2:15 PM

Forests are vital. They help prouce the oxygen we breathe and sequester the carbon that is warming the planet. But what happens when forest health starts to decline? This week’s Science special issue on forest health explores that question, and examines threats facing forests around the globe—both natural and human-caused.
